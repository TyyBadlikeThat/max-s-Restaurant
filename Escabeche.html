Escabeche

Escabeche is a delicate soup eaten with chicken on Sundays and other special occasions. This is a very simple soup to make the key is to keep it simple. A delicate, bright, acidic soup, typically eaten with corn tortillas, boiled and broiled chicken, and a glass of coke.

Escabeche Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken parts ; wings, legs, breast - whatever you want
  • 2 tablespoon Canola Oil
  • 5 cups Water
  • 1 clove Fresh garlic, peeled
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoon Chicken bouillon
  • 4 tablespoon pickled Jalapenos ; (but only the water. If you want it very spicy, add 2-3 small slices of jalapeno pepper
  • 5 tablespoons Cider Vinegar ; I use the apple vinegar
  • 1 cup Carrot ; sliced
  • 3 large white onion ; about 13 oz
  • 2 cups Water

    Instructions

  1. In a pan heat very well 2 TBS oil (aout 3-4 minutes). Add chicken to fry. Do not move in pan until is well fried on a side. Than flip it to the other side until well done (about 3-4 minutes each side until golden).
  2. In a seperate pot add water with 1 clove of garlic, 1/2 tsp dry oregano, 2 tbs Chicken bouillon.
  3. Leave few minutes to boil.
  4. Add 2 tbs JALAPENO juice. If you want it hotter, add more jalapenos.
  5. leave few minutes to boil
  6. add 3 tbs white vinegar and leave few more minutes to boil.
  7. Add chicken in water and taste the soup.
  8. add 1 cup carrots (sliced) and leave for a few minutes
  9. add little bit of oil (where you fried the chicken and scrub good the pot)
  10. Clean and cut in fine rounds fresh onion. huge white onion.
  11. If you taste it and if is not enough vinegar, add more until reach the taste you love.
  12. Add 2 cups of water in a separate pot (for the onion). You need to precook the onion to remove the strong smell.
  13. Slice all onion very thin . Boil only few minutes (3-4) and take the rings apart in same time.
  14. Drain the water and let onions rest.
  15. Leave the soup to boil for 10 more minutes. Add onion and immediately stop the fire.
  16. Serve with hot corn tortilla.
  17. Enjoy
