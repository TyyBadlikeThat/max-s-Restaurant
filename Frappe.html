Oreo Frappe

This deliciouse and refreshing frappe is excellent for those hot summer days. Three simple ingredients, a bleandeer and a cup is all you need

Oreo Frappe Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Ice
  • 1 Cup of milk
  • 2 tbsp Chocolate syrup
  • 3 Oreo cookies
  • 2 Scoop of ice cream
  • whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Place all the ingredients in a blender except the wip cream and blend at high speed for 2 minutes
  2. Serve in a cup
  3. Garnish with whipped cream and enjoy
